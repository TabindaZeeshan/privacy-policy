Request Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your AI Vocal Trainer account and associated personal data, you may request account deletion by contacting the developer.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email the developer using the contact email below.
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request.
  3. Include the email address associated with your account.

What Will Be Deleted

  • User account information
  • Student profiles associated with the account
  • Speech learning records and progress
  • Stored speech recordings (if applicable)

Data Retention

Account and associated personal data will be deleted within 30 days of receiving a verified deletion request, unless retention is required by law.
