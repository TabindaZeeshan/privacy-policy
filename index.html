Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

AI Vocal Trainer ("the App") is developed by Tabinda Zeeshan as part of a Final Year Project at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM). The application is designed to support speech development for preschool children by providing interactive speech-learning activities for use by teachers and parents.

Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information:

  • Name and email address of registered users.
  • Student profiles created by teachers or parents.
  • Speech practice results and learning progress.
  • Audio recordings captured during speech exercises.
  • Technical information required for application functionality.

Microphone Access

The App requests access to your device's microphone solely for recording speech during pronunciation and speech training activities. Microphone recordings are used only to evaluate speech performance and improve the learning experience.

Speech Recognition

The App uses Google Speech-to-Text services to convert recorded speech into text for pronunciation assessment. Audio data may be processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Firebase Services

The App uses Google Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore, to securely manage user accounts, store learning progress, and synchronize application data.

How We Use Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Provide speech learning activities.
  • Evaluate pronunciation performance.
  • Generate personalized learning progress.
  • Maintain user accounts.
  • Improve application performance.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent users' personal information. Information is shared only with trusted service providers required to operate the application, such as Google Firebase and Google Speech-to-Text.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for educational use by preschool children under the supervision of parents, teachers, or authorized educational staff. User accounts and student information should be managed by responsible adults.

Data Security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are implemented to protect user information. Data transmitted between the application and cloud services is encrypted using secure communication protocols.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase
  • Google Speech-to-Text API

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated effective date.
